Abstract

TILOTTAMA provides an innovative solution for menstrual waste management by leveraging AI-driven technology. The project aims to mitigate the environmental and health risks associated with menstrual waste disposal through efficient and targeted waste segregation practices. Our system optimizes logistical operations while ensuring safe and sustainable waste disposal methods, reducing contamination and promoting environmental sustainability.
